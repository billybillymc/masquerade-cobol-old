# Product Wedge

## Locked Decision

Workflow order is locked as:
1. **Impact analysis tied to business questions** (first-buyer wedge)
2. **Discovery/exploration**
3. **Migration pilot**

90-day KPI model is locked as:
- **Composite scorecard**
- **Primary metric: faster impact assessments**

Decision tradeoff summary:
- We keep a balanced value story across speed, risk, and understanding.
- We preserve one clear headline metric for execution focus and accountability.
- We accept more dashboard/reporting complexity than a single-KPI model.

## Why This Order

- Discovery alone is useful but can be perceived as "documentation tooling."
- Migration pilot alone is high-risk before trust is built.
- Impact analysis sits in the middle: immediate operational value and direct path to migration.

## 10-Minute Wow Demo

1. Ask: "Where do we calculate late fees?"
2. Show ranked answer with evidence spans and confidence.
3. Click through lineage of fee amount from input to output.
4. Change simulation: "If copybook field X changes, what breaks?"
5. Display ROI/equivalence readiness panel for top affected module.

## 90-Day Promise

Recommended promise: **safer change and faster comprehension**.

Targets:
- 50% faster rule discovery for analysts
- 40% faster impact assessments
- 30% fewer high-severity change incidents in pilot scope

Primary 90-day KPI:
- **Faster impact assessments** (time-to-answer reduction is the lead indicator)

Migration acceleration should be framed as a secondary outcome in this phase.

## Open Questions
