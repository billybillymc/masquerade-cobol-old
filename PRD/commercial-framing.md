# Commercial Framing Questions

## Locked Decision

Primary commercial motion is **hybrid packaging with exploration-first entry and migration upsell**.
Primary proof point is **impact accuracy**; secondary proof point is **validated modernization percentage**.
Primary pricing axis is **hybrid (platform + usage)**.
Default buyer/champion model is **CIO/Head of Core Systems Modernization + Mainframe Modernization Lead + Risk/Controls Lead**.

Decision tradeoff summary:
- We gain faster adoption by solving immediate comprehension/risk pain first.
- We preserve higher long-term revenue potential through reimplementation and migration workflows.
- We avoid forcing high-trust migration commitments before confidence is established.
- We accept a more complex product narrative that requires clear packaging and milestone-based upsell criteria.
- We align first proof to immediate buyer pain (safe change) while preserving a credible path to migration outcomes.
- We combine predictable platform revenue with value-linked usage expansion, at the cost of greater pricing-model complexity.
- We align decision authority, delivery ownership, and governance sign-off to reduce pilot-to-production friction.
